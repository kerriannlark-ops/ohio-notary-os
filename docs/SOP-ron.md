# SOP: RON

## Before session launch

1. Confirm the notary has active Ohio commission and active RON authorization.
2. Confirm the notary is physically located in Ohio.
3. Confirm the signer has internet, camera, microphone, and document access.
4. Confirm the platform supports credential analysis, identity proofing, recording, and tamper-evident journal records.

## During the session

1. Verify two-way live audio-video connection.
2. Complete credential analysis and identity proofing.
3. Review the document for completeness.
4. Apply the electronic certificate, signature, and seal.
5. Save the recording reference and journal entry before closing.

## After completion

1. Confirm the recording and journal metadata are retained.
2. Send invoice and follow-up.
3. Track end-of-authorization export obligations for the RON journal.
