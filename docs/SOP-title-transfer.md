# SOP: Title Transfer

## Mandatory caution

Ohio title work should stop immediately if buyer, seller, odometer, reassignment, or other required transfer fields are blank. This is a high-risk workflow and should always show a warning in the app.

## Checklist

1. Confirm the signer is the correct party on the title.
2. Confirm the assignment section is fully completed before notarization.
3. Confirm the signer understands you are not giving legal advice.
4. Verify acceptable ID and personal appearance.
5. Complete only the notarial certificate and your required notary fields.

## Refusal reasons to log

- blank title transfer fields
- signer absent
- mismatched ID
- unwilling signer
- apparent incapacity
