# SOP: General Notary

## Intake

1. Confirm service type, act count, signer count, location, and requested time.
2. Confirm the signer will personally appear.
3. Confirm the document is complete and unsigned.
4. Confirm ID method and whether travel fee was disclosed and accepted.

## At the appointment

1. Check signer willingness and apparent awareness.
2. Review the notarial certificate for completeness.
3. Verify identity by personal knowledge or acceptable evidence.
4. Witness the signature or acknowledgement as required.
5. Complete the venue, date, signature, commission expiration, and seal.

## After the appointment

1. Create a journal entry if using the traditional journal workflow.
2. Generate the invoice and record payment status.
3. Send the review request only for successfully completed appointments.
